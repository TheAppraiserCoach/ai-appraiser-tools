AI Appraiser Tools

Free, open-source tools for real estate appraisers — built by The Appraiser Coach.

What this is

A growing collection of practical tools for residential appraisers, built with AI and shared openly. Use them, fork them on GitHub, suggest improvements, build your own.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no signup, no data leaves your computer, no subscription required.

→

Depreciated Cost Calculator

Drop in an appraisal PDF, get suggested adjustment ranges for the construction-related elements of comparison (GLA, basement, garage) derived from the report's own Cost Approach. Auto-extracts the subject cost approach and the comp features from the sales grid.

 →

133-Step QC Reviewer (prompt template)

A pre-submission QC checklist for any 1004 / 1073 / 1004C / 1025 / 2055 / G-PAR report. Copy the prompt, paste into Claude.ai or ChatGPT, attach your appraisal PDF — get back severity-rated concerns with step references. Requires a paid Claude.ai or ChatGPT subscription.
