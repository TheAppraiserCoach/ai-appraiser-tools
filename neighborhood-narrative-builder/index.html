Neighborhood Narrative Builder

Give it the subject address + your trend boxes + your market numbers. Get suggested N/S/E/W boundaries and publication-ready neighborhood + market conditions narratives.

← All tools

1What this is

A prompt template that drafts three things at once:

  • Suggested boundaries — North, South, East, West major roads or natural features (rivers, ridges, parks, rail lines) that bound the neighborhood. Based on the subject address; verify locally before using.
  • Neighborhood Description narrative — fits the 1004 "Neighborhood Description" field. Covers location character, services, schools, market segment.
  • Market Conditions narrative — fits the 1004 "Market Conditions (including support for the above conclusions)" field. Supports your trend-box selections with the data you provide.

Output is third-person report voice. No marketing language ("desirable," "prime"), no hedge words, no AVM-style fluff. Drops in a [PLACEHOLDER] for any specific number you haven't given it — so the tool never fabricates data.

2What you give it

Required (must provide):

Subject property address (full)
The four neighborhood-character trend boxes you'd check on the form: urban/suburban/rural; built-up %; growth; values trend

Optional (the more you give, the less placeholder you'll see):

Demand/Supply box selection
Marketing-time box selection
School district name(s)
Dwelling price range (low – high)
Predominant occupancy (owner / tenant / vacant)
Land use % (1-unit / 2-4 / multi / commercial / other)
Market data: median price, DOM, absorption, 1004MC numbers
Specific neighborhood features (lakefront, golf, equestrian, etc.)
Any boundary preferences you already have
Boundary suggestions are starting points. The AI uses general knowledge about the area from its training data — accurate in most metros, weaker in rural areas. Always verify the suggested boundaries against your own market knowledge or a quick map check before using them in the report.

3How to use it

  1. Click Copy prompt to clipboard above.
  2. Open Claude.ai or ChatGPT.
  3. Paste the prompt.
  4. Under the prompt, type your inputs as plain text — at minimum the subject address and your four trend-box selections. Add as many of the optional fields as you have.
  5. Send. Wait 30–60 seconds.
  6. Review boundaries against a quick map check. Fill any [PLACEHOLDER] fields. Paste the narratives into TOTAL.
