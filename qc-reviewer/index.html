133-Step QC Reviewer

A pre-submission checklist prompt for residential appraisal reports. Use it with Claude.ai or ChatGPT.

← All tools

1What this is

This is a prompt template — not a downloadable program. It's 400+ lines of carefully written instructions that turn any modern AI into a residential appraisal QC reviewer.

The reviewer walks 133 numbered checks against your report (subject identification, comparable selection, adjustment grid, math, USPAP compliance, photos, and more), then returns severity-rated concerns sorted from most critical to least:

🔴 CRITICAL — likely lender/regulator rejection
🟠 MATERIAL — fix before submission
🟡 MINOR — clean-up only
🔵 INFO — context (e.g., AVM comparison)

Each concern cites the specific step number(s) it came from, explains why it matters, and recommends what to do about it. Supports 1004, 1073 (condo), 1004C (manufactured), 1025 (2–4 unit), 2055 (drive-by), and G-PAR.

Copy the prompt

Click below, paste into Claude.ai or ChatGPT, attach your appraisal PDF, hit send.

2How to use it

  1. Click Copy prompt to clipboard above.
  2. Open Claude.ai or ChatGPT in another tab.
  3. Start a new conversation. Paste the prompt into the message box (don't send yet).
  4. Attach your completed appraisal PDF using the paperclip / attachment icon.
  5. Send. Wait 30–90 seconds.
  6. Review the at-a-glance summary, then drill into the details for each concern.

Tip: keep your AI conversation open while you fix concerns in TOTAL — you can ask the AI follow-up questions on any specific concern ("show me the math you used for concern #3", "what's the exact USPAP citation here?", etc.).

3Which AI to use

You need a paid AI subscription that supports both vision (reading the photos in the report) and PDF attachments. Two solid choices:

Claude.ai (Pro plan)

Recommended. Pick the Opus or Sonnet model. About $20/month. Best results on the photo-quality and condition-rating checks.

ChatGPT (Plus plan)

Also works. Use GPT-4 / GPT-5 or whatever the current top vision-capable model is. About $20/month.

Free-tier AIs (free Claude, free ChatGPT) typically don't support PDF attachments or aren't vision-capable enough for the photo checks. Stick with paid plans.

4What to expect in the output

The reviewer produces a two-layer report: a quick summary (scannable in 30 seconds) and detailed entries below.

Reviewed all 133 checks. Format: Form 1004. Concerns identified below.

CRITICAL (1)
🔴 Subject GLA mismatch between sketch (1,484 sf) and grid (1,500 sf)

MATERIAL (3)
🟠 Comp 2 quality adjustment direction appears reversed
🟠 Date-of-sale adjustment missing despite stated declining market
🟠 Cost Approach effective age inconsistent with condition narrative

MINOR (2)
🟡 Census tract field empty
🟡 Borrower name capitalization inconsistent across pages

---
## Details

**CRITICAL** — Improvements / Cross-Section Consistency — Step(s) 66, 129
**Issue:** Sketch shows GLA of 1,484 sf; sales grid lists subject GLA as 1,500 sf.
**Why it matters:** GSE reviewers cross-check the sketch against the grid. A 16 sf discrepancy is small but will trigger a revision request.
**Recommendation:** Recompute GLA from the sketch dimensions and reconcile to a single number used everywhere in the report.

(...more concerns below...)

If the report has no concerns, the reviewer says so explicitly: "Reviewed all 133 checks. Format: [form]. No concerns identified."
