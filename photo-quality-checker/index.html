1What this is

A vision-required prompt template that turns Claude.ai or ChatGPT into a focused photo-only QC reviewer. It looks at every photo in your completed report and flags anything that would trigger an AMC kickback or fail an FHA/VA review.

Five categories of check:

📐 Required angles — front, rear, street-scene, all sides where practical, comp fronts
🏠 Interior coverage — kitchen, baths, primary bedroom, living areas, mechanicals (FHA/VA escalate this)
🎯 Condition match — C1–C6 rating vs visible condition in the photo (e.g., C2 claim with peeling paint)
🎨 Quality match — Q1–Q6 rating consistency across subject + comp photos
📷 Photo quality — blurry, dark, obstructed, crooked, watermarked stock images

Results come back severity-rated:

🔴 CRITICAL — required photo missing / clear rating mismatch
🟠 MATERIAL — weak photo affecting rating support
🟡 MINOR — usable but should be retaken next time
🔵 INFO — observation, not a defect

This complements the 133-Step QC Reviewer (which covers everything) — use this one when photos are your biggest worry, or when you've already fixed everything else and want a focused photo sweep.

2How to use it

  1. Click Copy prompt to clipboard above.
  2. Open Claude.ai or ChatGPT.
  3. Paste the prompt into the message box.
  4. Attach your completed appraisal PDF using the paperclip icon. Make sure photos render in the PDF (not just thumbnails).
  5. Send. Wait 60–120 seconds — vision reviews take a bit longer than text-only.
  6. Review the at-a-glance summary, fix the CRITICAL and MATERIAL items, ship.
Vision required. Use a model that can read images — Claude Opus or Sonnet, or GPT-4o / GPT-5. Free-tier models without vision will give you a useless report.

Heads-up: AI vision is good but not infallible. Treat every flagged item as "worth checking" rather than "definitely wrong." This is a triage tool, not a final reviewer.

## Details

🔴 **CRITICAL** — Interior coverage — Step P4
**Issue:** No photo of the secondary (hall) bathroom. FHA reports require an interior photo of every bathroom.
**Why it matters:** HUD 4000.1 requires photographic evidence of all bathrooms. Missing the photo triggers an automatic kickback during FHA review.
**Recommendation:** Add the missing photo before submission. If the bathroom was inaccessible at inspection, document the reason in the addendum.

🔴 **CRITICAL** — Condition match — Step P7
**Issue:** Subject front photo shows visible peeling paint on the soffits and porch trim. Grid lists subject as C2.
**Why it matters:** C2 means "limited physical depreciation" — peeling paint is deferred maintenance. Either the rating should drop to C3 or the deferred-maintenance is addressed in the narrative.
**Recommendation:** Either revise to C3 with a brief condition note, or expand the condition narrative to explain why the visible paint issue is cosmetic/insignificant relative to the overall condition.
